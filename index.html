COMPOSITION, ORIGIN AND PRODUCTION PATH

COMPOSITION

We work with monitoring programmes to ensure compliance with safety, health and quality standards for our engineers.

The Engineer 2.0 standard aims to minimise the environmental impact of Javascript manufacturing.
To that end, we have developed Web The Dev programme, which helps guarantee both that production processes are clean and that our engineers are safe and healthy.

ORIGIN

We work alongside our suppliers, workers, unions and international bodies to develop a supply chain that respects and promotes engineers skills, contributing to the United Nations Sustainable Web Development Goals.

Additionally, thanks to an ongoing collaboration with our suppliers, we have developed a tracking programme that allows us to determine where and how our products are being made.
